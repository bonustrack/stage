Stage Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026.

Stage is a private, open-source messenger built on the XMTP protocol.

What we do not do

No analytics or telemetry, no ads, no selling or sharing of personal data, and we cannot read your messages.

Your identity

Your account is an Ethereum wallet. No phone number, email, or sign-up info. Private keys are generated and stored on your device in its secure hardware-backed keystore and never leave it.

Your messages

End-to-end encrypted via XMTP (MLS), decryptable only by conversation participants. Attachments are encrypted on your device before upload; only participants hold the keys.

Notifications

If enabled, your device notification token, wallet address and XMTP inbox id are registered with our notification service. Notifications are contentless - the service never receives message contents. Turning notifications off removes your token.

Data deletion

Account, keys and history live on your device and are removed on uninstall. Turning notifications off (or uninstalling) removes your push token. Other requests: contact below.

Blockchain

Crypto transactions are recorded on public blockchains via public RPC providers - public by nature, not collected by Stage.

Open source

MIT licensed, full source publicly auditable.
