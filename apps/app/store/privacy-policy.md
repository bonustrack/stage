# Stage Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-05

This privacy policy describes how the Stage mobile app ("Stage", "the app",
"we") handles your information. Stage is a wallet-native, end-to-end encrypted
messenger built on the open XMTP protocol and the Ethereum network.

For questions, contact: tony@bonustrack.co

## Our approach

Stage is designed so that we hold as little of your data as technically
possible. You sign in with your own Ethereum wallet, your messages are
end-to-end encrypted, and your private keys never leave your device. We do not
operate an account system that stores your personal profile, and we cannot read
your messages.

## Information handled by the app

### Wallet address and identity
Your identity in Stage is your Ethereum wallet address (and any associated ENS
name). This address is used to send and receive messages and is, by the nature
of messaging, visible to the people you communicate with and to the XMTP network
that routes your messages.

### Messages and message content
Messages — including text, images, voice messages, and shared location — are
end-to-end encrypted using the XMTP protocol before they leave your device. Only
you and your intended recipients hold the keys to decrypt them. The relays that
carry your messages, and the operators of Stage, cannot read their content.

### Private keys
Your wallet's private keys are generated and/or stored on your device using the
platform's secure storage (Android Keystore via secure-store). We never receive,
transmit, or have custody of your private keys or your funds. Stage is fully
self-custodial.

### Photos, audio, and location
Stage accesses your photos, microphone, or location only when you explicitly
choose to attach an image, record a voice message, or share your location in a
conversation. These are sent end-to-end encrypted to your chosen recipients and
are not collected for any other purpose.

### Push notifications
To alert you to new messages, the app registers a push notification token with
our notification service. Our notification design is privacy-preserving: we do
not send your plaintext message content to third-party push servers.

## What we do not do

- We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
- We do not have custody of your funds or private keys.
- We do not sell your data.
- We do not use third-party advertising or ad-tracking SDKs.
- We do not upload your contacts.
- We cannot read the content of your end-to-end encrypted messages.

## Third-party infrastructure

Stage relies on open, decentralized infrastructure:

- The XMTP network relays your end-to-end encrypted messages.
- The Ethereum network and ENS resolve onchain identities and names.
- Public services (e.g. avatar/name resolution) may receive your public wallet
  address in order to display names and avatars.

These networks are public by design; information you publish onchain or send
over a public network may be visible to others.

## Data retention and deletion

Your keys and local data live on your device. Uninstalling Stage removes this
local data. Because your identity is your own wallet and your messages are
end-to-end encrypted, we do not maintain a server-side copy of your message
content to delete. For any operator-side requests, contact tony@bonustrack.co.

## Children

Stage is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data
from them.
