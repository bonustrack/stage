## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Metro is a private messenger and wallet companion for the decentralized web. Chat
wallet-to-wallet over the open XMTP network, with every message end-to-end
encrypted — no phone number, no email, just your wallet.

Key features:

• End-to-end encrypted messaging — conversations are secured with the XMTP
  protocol's E2E encryption. Only you and the people you talk to can read them.

• Wallet-native identity — sign in with your existing Ethereum wallet. Your
  address is your handle; no accounts to create, no personal data to hand over.

• Group and direct chats — start 1:1 conversations or group channels, share
  images, voice messages, and your location, and react to messages.

• Push notifications — get notified about new messages. Notifications are
  privacy-preserving and contentless by default.

• Open and portable — built on XMTP, an open messaging protocol. Your identity
  and conversations are not locked to Metro.

Metro does not sell your data. It does not run ads. Your messages are encrypted
end-to-end and Metro's servers never have the keys to read them.
