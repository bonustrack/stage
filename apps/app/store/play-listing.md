## Full description
Stage is a wallet-native messenger built on XMTP, the open end-to-end encrypted
messaging protocol for Ethereum addresses. Sign in with your wallet and your
inbox follows you across apps — no phone number, no email, no central server
holding your messages.

What you can do with Stage:

- Private 1:1 and group chats, end-to-end encrypted with XMTP. Only you and the
  people you talk to can read your messages.
- Message any Ethereum address or ENS name directly — your contacts are your
  onchain identities, not a proprietary social graph.
- A built-in self-custody wallet: your keys stay on your device in secure
  storage. Stage never has custody of your funds or your private keys.
- Rich messaging: text, images, voice messages, and location sharing.
- ENS names and wallet avatars resolve automatically so conversations are
  readable, not hexadecimal.
- Push notifications for new messages, with a privacy-preserving design that
  keeps message content off third-party servers.

Stage is for people who want a messenger that respects ownership: of your
identity, your conversations, and your assets. Because it is built on open
protocols (XMTP and Ethereum), your inbox is portable and interoperable with
the wider XMTP ecosystem.

Stage is currently in internal testing.
