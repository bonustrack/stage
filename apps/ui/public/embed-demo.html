Metro embed widget — demo

This page loads /embed.js with a sample data-conv-id. Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right to expand the messenger.

To embed Metro on your own site, paste a single <script> tag:

<script
  src="https://metro.box/embed.js"
  data-conv-id="YOUR_CONV_ID"
  data-title="Chat with us"
  defer
></script>

The widget is lazy: the iframe only boots when the visitor clicks the button.
